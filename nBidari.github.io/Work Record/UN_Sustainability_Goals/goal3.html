The 3rd UN Sustainability Goal is Good Health and Well-Being. The whole point is to help improve the health of people around the world. Both mental and physical health are included. For example, mental health could mean the different moods you are in when you are hungry, thirsty, or when you had a bad day and you're feeling down. Physical health could be just an everyday sickness like the cold, or the flu. Although you may have access to medicine, like antibiotics, to help you get better, not everyone has that luxury. The UN is helping solve that problem. Malaria, a sickness spread by pesky mosquitos, can kill people in rural areas of Africa, however, we have successfully halved those deaths! The deaths of children throughout third world countries have also declined.

The UN hopes to increase access to medicine, doctors, nurses, medicinal facilities, and provide those services, equally!

Have you ever had the cold, or the flu, and you just couldn't stop coughing? Have you ever felt so out of breath that you felt like you needed more air or you'd pass out? Well, that's the reality for millions worldwide. Household air pollution is one of those causes. It could be anywhere from leaving the stove on, and the smoke making them cough a lot, or a lot of pollen (asthma).
